Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to RGL Tasks ("we," "our," or "the App"). High importance is placed on your privacy and the protection of your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Telegram Mini App.

2. Information We Collect

While using RGL Tasks, we may collect certain information provided by the Telegram platform:

  • Telegram Profile Information: This includes your user ID, first name, last name, username, and profile photo URL.
  • App Activity: We track your task completion progress, RGL coin balance, and referral data within the App.
  • Wallet Information: If you connect your TON wallet via TonConnect, we will have access to your public wallet address. We never have access to your private keys or seed phrases.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality.
  • To track and verify task completions for RGL reward distribution.
  • To process withdrawal requests to your TON wallet.
  • To prevent fraud, multiple accounts, and abuse of the reward system.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your local app state (like balance and completed tasks) is stored on your device using LocalStorage. We may also store verification data on our secure backend servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but please be aware that no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with third-party services:

  • Telegram Messenger: The platform hosting the Mini App.
  • TON Blockchain: Facilitating coin withdrawals via TonConnect.

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access the data we hold about you and to request its deletion. If you wish to delete your data, please contact us or remove the Mini App from your Telegram account and clear your local storage.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our official Telegram channel.
